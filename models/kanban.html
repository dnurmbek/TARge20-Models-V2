Kanban: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
☆ Improves work flow ★ Outdated Kanban board that can lead to issues in the development
☆ Minimizes time spent ★ The implementation of the system may result in poor quality outputs.
☆ Reduces waste from process ★ As the tasks are continuously shifted between the columns of kanban board, the prediction of specific timelines for completion of tasks or activities becomes difficult.
