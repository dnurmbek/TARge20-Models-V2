SCRUM: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
☆ Large projects are divided into easily manageable sprints ★ Scrum often leads to scope creep, due to the lack of a definite end-date.
☆ The team gets clear visibility through scrum meetings ★ The chances of project failure are high if individuals aren't very committed or cooperative.
☆ Short sprints enable changes based on feedback a lot more easily ★ Adopting the Scrum framework in large teams is challenging.
