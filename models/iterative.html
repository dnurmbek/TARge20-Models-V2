Iterative model: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
☆ Some working functionality can be developed early in the software development life cycle. ★ More resources may be required.
☆ It is easily adaptable to the ever changing needs of the project as well as the client. ★ More management attention is required.
☆ It is more cost effective to change the scope or requirements in Iterative model. ★ It is not suitable for smaller projects.
